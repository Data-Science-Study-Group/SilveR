Analysis description

The data were analysed using a 3-way ANCOVA approach, with Treat1, Treat2 and Treat3 as the treatment factors and Cov 1 as the covariate.This was followed by all pairwise comparisons between the predicted means of the Treat1 factor using Bonferroni's procedure. The response was Loge transformed prior to analysis to stabilise the variance.

For more information on the theoretical approaches that are implemented within this module, see Bate and Clark (2014).
