Analysis description

The data were analysed using a 3-way ANOVA approach, with Treat9, Treat10 and Treat11 as the treatment factors and Block3 as a blocking factor. This was followed by comparisons of the predicted means of the Treat9 factor back to the control group mean using Hommel's procedure. This was followed by all pairwise comparisons between the predicted means of the Treat9 factor using Hommel's procedure.

For more information on the theoretical approaches that are implemented within this module, see Bate and Clark (2014).
