Analysis description

The data were analysed using a 3-way ANCOVA approach, with Treat10, Treat9 and Treat11 as the treatment factors, Block3 and Block4 as the blocking factors and Cov6 as the covariate.This was followed by comparisons of the predicted means of the Treat10 factor back to the control group mean using Dunnett's procedure. The response was ArcSine transformed prior to analysis to stabilise the variance.

For more information on the theoretical approaches that are implemented within this module, see Bate and Clark (2014).
