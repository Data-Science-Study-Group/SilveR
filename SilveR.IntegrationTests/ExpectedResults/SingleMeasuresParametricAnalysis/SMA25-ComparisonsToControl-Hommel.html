Analysis description

The data were analysed using a 1-way ANOVA approach, with Treat2 as the treatment factor and Blo ck1 as a blocking factor. This was followed by comparisons of the predicted means of the Treat2 factor back to the control group mean using Hommel's procedure.

For more information on the theoretical approaches that are implemented within this module, see Bate and Clark (2014).
