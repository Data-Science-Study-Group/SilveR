Analysis description

The data were analysed using a 1-way ANCOVA approach, with Treat1 as the treatment factor and Cov 1 as the covariate.This was followed by Planned Comparisons of the predicted means to compare the levels of the Treat1 factor. The response was Square Root transformed prior to analysis to stabilise the variance.

For more information on the theoretical approaches that are implemented within this module, see Bate and Clark (2014).
