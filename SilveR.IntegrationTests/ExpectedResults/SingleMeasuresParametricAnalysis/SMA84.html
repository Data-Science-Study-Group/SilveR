Analysis description

The data were analysed using a 2-way ANCOVA approach, with Treat1 and Treat2 as the treatment factors, Blo ck1 as a blocking factor and Cov 1 and Cov1a as the covariates.This was followed by comparisons of the predicted means of the Treat1 factor back to the control group mean using Dunnett's procedure. This was followed by all pairwise comparisons between the predicted means of the Treat1 factor using Tukey's procedure. The response was Square Root transformed prior to analysis to stabilise the variance.

For more information on the theoretical approaches that are implemented within this module, see Bate and Clark (2014).
